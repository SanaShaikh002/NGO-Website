About Us

Navi Dish Swam Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to creating a sustainable and equitable future. Since 2010, we've impacted thousands of lives through education, health, environment, and empowerment programs.

Our Journey

  • 2010: Started in Pune with just 3 volunteers.
  • 2013: Launched mobile classrooms for slum children.
  • 2017: Crossed 100+ successful health camps.
  • 2020: Delivered 10,000+ food kits during COVID lockdown.
  • 2024: Initiated digital empowerment workshops for women.

Vision

A world where every person has the right to live with dignity, access opportunities, and thrive.

Mission

To empower marginalized communities by providing tools, knowledge, and support needed to build a better future.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We serve with kindness and respect.
  • Integrity: We work with honesty and transparency.
  • Inclusiveness: We welcome everyone, without discrimination.
  • Empowerment: We focus on long-term transformation.
